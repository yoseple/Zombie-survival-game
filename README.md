# DEAD ROADS - Master Game Design Document

Welcome to the comprehensive Game Design Document for **DEAD ROADS**, a premium AAA mobile narrative RPG. 

Due to the massive scale and extreme detail of this design, the documentation has been split into dedicated modules. Please review the following files in order to understand the complete vision, architecture, and systems of the game.

## Directory Index

1. **[Executive Summary & Pillars](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/01_Executive_Summary_and_Pillars.md)**
   * High-level overview, market positioning, USPs, competitive analysis, player fantasy, and the core design pillars that drive all development.

2. **[World Design](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/02_World_Design.md)**
   * The universe, pathogen origins, timeline of the collapse, geographical regions, major locations, and environmental storytelling mechanics.

3. **[Story Structure](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/03_Story_Structure.md)**
   * The overarching 4-season narrative arc, detailing themes, conflicts, antagonists, and major emotional beats for each season.

4. **[Main Character Systems](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/04_Character_Systems.md)**
   * Player customization, backgrounds, personality archetypes, skill progression, and the deep leadership/stress mechanics.

5. **[Survivor & Relationship Systems](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/05_Survivor_and_Relationship_Systems.md)**
   * The 50 major survivors architecture, recruitment, trust scores, mutiny, romance, and emergent social simulation.

6. **[Choice & Consequence System](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/06_Choice_and_Consequence_System.md)**
   * The technical and narrative logic of the Consequence Engine, tracking how micro and macro decisions ripple across episodes and seasons.

7. **[Core Gameplay Systems](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/07_Gameplay_Systems.md)**
   * Moment-to-moment gameplay loops, including the strict Episode Template, the tactical mobile-first combat system, the noise mechanics, and the evolutionary Zombie System.

8. **[Economy & Infrastructure](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/08_Base_and_Resource_Systems.md)**
   * The macro-management meta-game: Base building, facility upgrades, the punishing economy of scarcity, and inventory/crafting mechanics.

9. **[Factions, Quests, & Progression](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/09_Factions_Quests_Progression.md)**
   * Detailed breakdown of the 10 major geopolitical factions, quest architecture, and the multi-layered progression loops holding the game together.

10. **[Endgame, Art, & Audio](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/10_Endgame_Art_Audio.md)**
    * The architecture of the 20 unique branching endings, combined with the comprehensive visual style, color scripting, and acoustic direction.

11. **[Tech Specs, UI, & Final Vision](DEAD_ROADS_GDD/11_UI_Tech_Roadmap_Risks.md)**
    * User Interface design, Unity 6 technical architecture, performance budgets, the live-ops roadmap, risk analysis, and the concluding vision statement.
